EV6

Despite its sleek and compact design, the EV6 offers a spacious and luxurious interior capturing our attention with its cool and sophisticated appeal. The platform's long wheelbase maximizes cabin space, providing ample legroom and headroom for both the driver and passengers.

The Kia EV6 is praised for its powerful acceleration, smooth handling, and overall driving experience. You will appreciate the instant torque provided by the electric motors, making city driving effortless and highway cruising exhilarating.

The striking exterior finish enhances its modern and futuristic design, reflecting the EV6's commitment to sustainability and style combining elegance and innovation seamlessly, providing a serene driving experience that elevates your journey. Prepare to be impressed by its impressive performance and advanced features.

Kia's cutting-edge electric powertrain technology

Its top-of-the-line GT variant can accelerate from 0 to 100 kilometers per hour in a breathtaking 3.5 seconds, thanks to its dual electric motor setup that produces a combined power output of 430 kW (577 horsepower).

The EV6 offers all-wheel drive (AWD) capability, distributing power efficiently between the front and rear wheels. This feature ensures exceptional traction and stability, especially during challenging road conditions.
