EV6 - Revolutionizing Electric Mobility

Vehicle-to-Load - V2L

Allows you to harness the power of your vehicle's battery to charge various devices or even power your home. With the EV6's V2L capabilities, you can effortlessly plug in and charge electronic equipment, from laptops to electric bicycles, power outages or camping trips, the EV6 can act as a mobile power source, providing electricity for your essential appliances.

Enhanced Regenerative Braking System

When you apply the brakes or lift your foot off the accelerator, the EV6's regenerative braking system harnesses the kinetic energy and converts it into electrical energy, which is then used to recharge the vehicle's battery. Extending the driving range by utilizing energy, resulting in improved longevity and reduced maintenance costs.

Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS)

The EV6 is equipped with an intelligent feature to enhance your driving experience, providing assistance, and reducing the risk of accidents. The EV6's ADAS includes features such as Highway Driving Assist (HDA), which combines adaptive cruise control and lane-keeping assist to provide a smooth and safe driving experience on highways.

Dual Panaromic Screens

The EV6 is equipped with dual panoramic screens, creating an immersive and intuitive interface for both driver and passengers. The high-resolution displays provide access to vital vehicle information, entertainment options, and seamless smartphone integration. Enabling you to effortlessly control navigation, audio, and climate settings, ensuring a connected and engaging driving experience.

Ultra-Fast Charging

The EV6 can charge from 10% to 80% in just 18 minutes, minimizing downtime and maximizing convenience during your journeys. Whether you're embarking on a long road trip or running errands around the city, the EV6's fast-charging capability ensures you spend more time on the road and less time waiting at charging stations.

Augmented Reality HUD

This advanced technology projects essential driving information onto the windshield, overlaying it seamlessly onto the real-world view. The intuitive and immersive AR HUD enhances safety, minimizes distractions, and provides a futuristic driving experience that merges technology with the real world.
